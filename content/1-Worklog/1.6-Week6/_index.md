### Week 6 Objectives:
* Understand the theory and practice of managing resources using Tags and Resource Groups.
* Understand the theory and practice of AWS Identity and Access Management (IAM).
* Understand how to manage access to the EC2 service through Resource Tags combined with IAM.
* Understand and practice how to limit a user's permissions using IAM Permission Boundary.
* Understand the theory and practice of encrypting data at rest with AWS Key Management Service (KMS).
