Throughout my internship at Amazon Web Services Vietnam Company Limited, from June 8, 2026 to July 5, 2026, I had the opportunity to experience a real working environment, apply the knowledge I had gained at school to actual work, and learn additional knowledge and skills that my academic program had not fully covered.
 
During this time, I learned about many AWS cloud services and took part in the Globalmart team project focused on CI/CD, which helped me gradually improve skills such as programming, problem analysis, communication, and gaining deeper knowledge of AWS services. This was not only a process of professional training but also an opportunity to better understand professional working conduct within a real organization.
 
In terms of attitude and work conduct, I always tried to complete assigned tasks well, follow the general rules and regulations, and proactively communicate with colleagues and my mentor to make timely adjustments, in order to ensure the best possible work performance.
